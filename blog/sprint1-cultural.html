Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Heya, welcome to my blog. My core values are self-improvement, work ethic and family which I’ll elaborate on whilst adding to this blog over the span of me attending to Dev Academy! The first core value being self-improvement, I think there’s multiple ways to interpret what self improvement means and other people will have different views to what that means. In my opinion, self-improvement is the sense of being the best person I know I can be and bettering myself in anyway possible whether it be knowledge or just treating others better on a daily basis. These are the things I find most important in life and know that it’ll help me through whatever career I choose to take down the line. Second core value: Work ethic! Similar to the first value of mine, I believe that maintaining a good work ethic and working my hardest in every situation is key to (also being the best I can be) feeling productive and good about myself whenever completing given tasks or even tasks I’ve given myself. It also shows others around me that I am capable of doing what I am told, and that I am not afraid to give something a shot whether I’m familiar with it or not. And finally the third core value would be family, which is because family and friends are what have shaped me into the person I am today, and give me the most valuable reason to work hard and show me a representation of what to strive for everyday. I’ve never really had an experience where I’ve been trying to work with others and there’s been tension or resistance, I’ve found that working my hardest helps others also want to work hard. My strengths that will help with my learning journey is how persistent I am. Even through the first sprint I’ve been stuck a few times and came back multiple times to try solve whatever’s been interrupting my flow.
